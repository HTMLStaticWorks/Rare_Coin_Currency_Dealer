Our Committment & Policies

Authenticity Guarantee

All items sold by RareCoin are guaranteed to be authentic. We exclusively deal with items certified by PCGS, NGC, or PMG. If any item is found to be non-authentic by these major services, we provide a 100% immediate refund including all shipping and insurance costs.

Insured Shipping Policy

We understand the value of your investment. Every shipment is fully insured for its full replacement value. High-value orders over $50,000 are shipped via specialized secure couriers. A signature from the authorized recipient is always required upon delivery.

Returns & Restocking

We offer a 7-day inspection period for all non-bullion items. Returns must be in their original, unopened certification holders. A 5% restocking fee may apply to certain high-value auctions or specialized acquisitions. Bullion items are subject to market volatility and are non-returnable.
