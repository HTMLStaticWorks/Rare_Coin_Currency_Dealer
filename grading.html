The Gold Standard for Grading

Understanding how rare assets are evaluated and protected.

PCGS Authentication

Professional Coin Grading Service (PCGS) is the industry's most respected third-party grading service. We exclusively handle PCGS certified items for high-value American coinage.

  • Guaranteed authenticity
  • Standardized grading scale (1-70)
  • Tamp-evident sonic-sealed holders

NGC Certification

Numismatic Guaranty Company (NGC) provides world-class grading for both coins and paper currency. Their expertise is unmatched in world and ancient numismatics.

  • Expert visual analysis
  • Secure conservation services
  • Online certification lookup

PMG Paper Money

Paper Money Guaranty (PMG) is the leader in third-party paper money grading. We ensure all rare banknotes are PMG certified for maximum protection.

  • Note holder longevity
  • Counterfeit detection
  • Comprehensive grading

Ancient Coin Evaluation

Specialized evaluation for Greek, Roman, and Byzantine coinage. We verify provenance and historical context for every ancient asset.

  • Historical verification
  • Grading by strike/surface
  • Rarity assessment

Conservation Services

Professional stabilization and cleaning to preserve the long-term value of your collection without damaging the surface.

  • PVC removal
  • Environmental protection
  • Museum-quality care

Provenance Research

Tracing the lineage of your assets back to their original discovery or auction appearances to build a robust pedigree.

  • Auction record search
  • Pedigree documentation
  • Historical tracing

Insurance Appraisal

Certified valuations for high-value collections to ensure proper insurance coverage and replacement value protection.

  • Full market analysis
  • Replacement cost evaluation
  • Formal documentation

Secure Vaulting

Off-site specialized storage for your most valuable assets with 24/7 security and climate control.

  • Fully insured storage
  • Climate-neutral environment
  • Specialized security

Auction Representation

Anonymized representation at major global auctions to secure rare pieces for your private collection.

  • Professional bidding
  • Market strategy
  • Confidentiality

Private Treaty Sales

Facilitating discreet, high-value transfers between high-net-worth collectors outside of public auctions.

  • Complete discretion
  • Targeted matching
  • Secured escrow

AU 50-58 (About Uncirculated)Slight wear on the highest design points.
XF 40-45 (Extremely Fine)Light wear over high design areas.
VF 20-35 (Very Fine)Moderate even wear throughout.
