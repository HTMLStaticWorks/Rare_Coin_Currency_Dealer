The Sheldon Scale

The industry standard for grading commercial and rare assets.

MS 60-70 Mint State — A coin that has never been in circulation, showing full luster. MS-70 is absolute perfection.
AU 50-58 About Uncirculated — Slight wear on the highest design points, retains most original surface luster.
XF 40-45 Extremely Fine — Light wear over high design areas; all detail remains sharp and clearly visible.
VF 20-35 Very Fine — Moderate even wear throughout the design; major features remain well-defined but flattened.
F 12-15 Fine — Deep wear; most design details are worn flat, but the major devices are clear.
